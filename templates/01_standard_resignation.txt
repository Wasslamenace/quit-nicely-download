TEMPLATE 1: STANDARD RESIGNATION (2 WEEKS NOTICE)

Subject: Resignation – [Your Name]

Dear [Manager Name],

I am writing to formally resign from my position as [Job Title] at [Company Name], effective [Last Day – 2 weeks from today].

This decision was not made lightly. I have greatly valued my time here, particularly [specific experience: project, team collaboration, skill development]. However, I have accepted a position that aligns more closely with my long-term career goals.

I am committed to ensuring a smooth transition. Over the next two weeks, I will:
- Complete [ongoing project/task]
- Document processes for [responsibility area]
- Train [successor or team member] on [key responsibilities]

Thank you for the opportunities and support during my tenure. I wish [Company Name] continued success.

Best regards,  
[Your Name]

---
HOW TO USE:
1. Replace all [placeholders] with your details
2. Copy-paste into email
3. Send to your manager
4. BCC yourself (paper trail)
5. Schedule meeting same day
