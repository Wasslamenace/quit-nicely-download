AI BONUS PROMPT #3: COUNTEROFFER RESPONSE

Copy this into ChatGPT or Claude:

---

I resigned, and my manager offered me a counteroffer:
- What they offered: [Higher salary / promotion / remote work / equity / better team / etc.]

My initial decision to leave was based on:
- Reason 1: [e.g., burnout / better opportunity elsewhere / toxic culture / lack of growth]
- Reason 2: [e.g., compensation / work-life balance / career advancement]

Generate:
1. A diplomatic way to DECLINE the counteroffer (if I want to leave anyway)
2. A way to ACCEPT if I'm genuinely interested (without seeming desperate)
3. Questions to ask to evaluate if the counteroffer is genuine or just desperation

---

HOW TO USE:
1. Fill in what they offered and why you originally wanted to leave
2. Paste into ChatGPT or Claude
3. Get diplomatic scripts for both scenarios
4. Decide: accept or decline?
5. Respond professionally

PRO TIP: 
Statistics show 50-80% of people who accept counteroffers leave within 6-12 months anyway. 
Ask yourself: "Does this counteroffer fix the REAL reason I wanted to leave?"
