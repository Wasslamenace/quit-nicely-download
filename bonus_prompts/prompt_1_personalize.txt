AI BONUS PROMPT #1: PERSONALIZE STANDARD TEMPLATE

Copy this into ChatGPT or Claude:

---

I'm resigning from my job as [Job Title] at [Company Name]. Here's my situation:
- Reason for leaving: [new job / burnout / relocation / toxic environment / career pivot / etc.]
- Relationship with manager: [good / neutral / tense]
- Notice period: [2 weeks / 30 days / immediate]
- Key achievements: [list 2-3 specific wins you had at the company]

Generate a professional resignation email that:
1. Keeps a [warm / neutral / formal] tone
2. Mentions my achievements tactfully (without bragging)
3. Offers to help with transition
4. Is concise (under 200 words)

---

HOW TO USE:
1. Replace all [placeholders] with your actual situation
2. Paste into ChatGPT or Claude
3. Review the generated email
4. Adjust tone/wording if needed
5. Send!
